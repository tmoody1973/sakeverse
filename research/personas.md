# Sakécosm User Personas

## Primary Personas

### 1. Wine Enthusiast → Sake Curious
**Name:** Sarah, 34  
**Role:** Marketing Manager  
**Location:** San Francisco, CA

**Background:**
- WSET Level 2 certified, regularly attends wine tastings
- Loves Pinot Noir, Burgundy, natural wines
- Tried sake once at a sushi restaurant, found it confusing
- Shops at wine.com, Vivino, local wine shops

**Goals:**

**How Sakécosm Helps:**
- Kiki translates "I like Pinot Noir" → "Try aged Junmai"

- Personalized recommendations based on wine preferences

**Acquisition Channel:** Wine blogs, sommelier Instagram, r/wine

## Key Insights

1. **Wine-to-Sake Bridge is Core Value Prop** - Sarah persona represents largest opportunity
2. **Tippsy Partnership Critical** - Jennifer persona is pre-qualified buyer
3. **B2B is Future Revenue** - Michelle/David personas have high LTV but need different features
4. **Gamification Appeals to Marcus** - Younger users want progression systems
5. **Voice-First Differentiates** - All personas benefit from conversational interface
